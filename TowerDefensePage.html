Tower Defense

About the Project

As a group, we chose to apply accesibility functions to a game from open-source code rather then make a game from scratch.

At the start, there was a lot to fix before starting. The spiders spawning maxed at only five or six spiders at wave five, there was no functionality for turret upgrades, and some of the upgrades were not even upgrades. And so, I ended up fixing these things too.

Color & Contrast

For the visual aspect of accessibility, I created the option to change the text color and contrast for those with colorblindness. The contrast can also help those with visual impairments, such as when in direct sunlight.

Difficulty

For the difficulty, it is there for new players and returning players. I changed the turret prices, the player's lives, and the spiders speed and health to accomplish this.
